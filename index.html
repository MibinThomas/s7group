Who We Are

S7 Group was founded in Dubai, by people who’ve witnessed the city rise and who are committed to its future. Guided by the legacy of Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan, and aligned with the vision of his Highness Sheikh Mohamed bin Zayed Al Nahyan and His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, we work to bring the UAE’s ambitions to life.

At our core Sustainability, Sports, and Space — the three pillars that define the “S” in S7. The 7 reflects the elements that drive our purpose and growth

We bring together governments, corporates, and communities, delivering projects that contribute to the nation’s future.
